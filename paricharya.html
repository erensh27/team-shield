Paricharya 2.0

Advanced Hygienic Bed for Bedridden Patients

Introduction

Paricharya 2.0 is designed to restore hygiene, dignity, and comfort for bedridden patients. It features a circular mattress cut-out system, automated flushing (future), and multilingual voice control — all engineered to reduce caregiver dependency.

The Problems

  • Pain & discomfort from traditional bedpans.
  • Hygiene issues leading to infections or bedsores.
  • Lack of caregivers — 30% of patients urgently require assistance.
  • Fear, discomfort, and reduced mobility.

Design Features

  • Circular Mattress Cut-Out: Opens downward smoothly.
  • Automated Flush System: Self-cleaning (future integration).
  • Voice-Controlled: Commands like “Open”, “Close” in multiple languages.
  • Durability: Water-resistant, detachable parts for easy cleaning.

How It Works

  1. User says “open” → mattress section retracts.
  2. Waste passage aligns with flushing mechanism.
  3. Self-cleaning (future module) activates.
  4. “Close” command restores mattress shape.

Applications

  • Home care for elderly or long-term patients.
  • Hospitals & nursing homes.
  • Rehabilitation centers.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Cost: Use of affordable materials + scalable design.
  • Reliability: Testing + robust engineering.
  • Language support: Software updates → more dialects.
