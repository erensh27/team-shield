Rakshak-X

The Cyclone & Tornado Safety Vehicle

Overview

Rakshak-X is an advanced cyclone and tornado safety car designed to increase survival rates during natural disasters. The concept is built around early detection, environmental monitoring, and automated safety responses.

The Problems We Target

  • Carbon Monoxide Poisoning: A major silent killer worldwide.
  • Cyclones & Tornadoes: Over 15,000 global deaths in 2023 alone.
  • Vehicle Fires: High annual casualties in both India and the USA.
  • General Road Accidents: 1.35 million fatalities annually.

Key Features

  • Object Detection: Ultrasonic sensor halts for debris during storms.
  • Tornado Detection: BMP180 monitors pressure drops & temperature.
  • Gas Leak Detector: MQ-2 alerts for hazardous gas exposure.
  • Real-Time Dashboard: Live safety data displayed every 4 seconds.

Emergency Assistance

  • Dedicated emergency button for quick rescue requests.
  • Plans for auto-notifying hospitals & authorities.

Future Prospects

  • Ground Suction System: Prevents vehicle lift during extreme winds.
  • Advanced Materials: Stronger chassis for impact resistance.
  • AI Vision: ML-based object & tornado pattern detection.

Achievements

  • Qualified for IHFC 2024 Nationals (IIT Delhi).
  • Featured & interviewed on DD National.
