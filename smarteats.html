SmartEats

Your AI-Driven Personal Food & Health Assistant

What is SmartEats?

SmartEats is an AI-powered meal and lifestyle assistant designed to help users make healthier food choices without sacrificing taste. It learns from user preferences, available ingredients, diet goals, and feedback to generate highly personalised meal recommendations.

Why SmartEats?

  • India’s obesity and lifestyle disease cases are rising sharply.
  • People want healthy meals but don’t know what to cook.
  • Existing recipe apps don’t personalise deeply enough.

Core Features

  • AI Meal Generator: Recipes based on ingredients you already have.
  • Healthy Swaps: Smarter alternatives to sugars, oils, snacks.
  • Nutrition Insights: Calories, macros, and suitability for your goals.
  • Adaptive Learning: Becomes smarter as you use it.
  • Diet Modes: Vegan, low-sugar, high protein, weight loss, muscle gain.
